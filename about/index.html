--- layout: default title: About Tamir Dresher ---

Tamir Dresher is the Head of Architecture at Payoneer - the leading FinTech company for B2B cross-border payments.

Tamir is a technology addict and helped organization and startups create successful scalable distributed systems with .NET. He is a prominent member of Israel’s Microsoft developers community and was rewarded Microsoft MVP for his contributions as a conferences speaker, organizing developer-community events, and authoring the books “Rx.NET in Action” (published by Manning) and “Hands-On Full-Stack Web Development with ASP.NET Core” (published by Packt). Tamir loves teaching as much as he loves programming and enjoys mentoring student as part of his role as software engineering professor at the Ruppin Academic Center and a speaker in international conferences. You can reach out to him via Twitter @tamir_dresher
